How Solar Power Works

  1. Sunlight Hits Panels: Solar panels on your rooftop absorb sunlight with photovoltaic (PV) cells.
  2. DC Electricity is Created: The PV cells convert the sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity.
  3. Inverter Converts Electricity: A solar inverter converts the DC electricity into alternating current (AC) electricity, which is the type used in your home.
  4. Power Your Home: The AC electricity flows into your home's electrical panel to power your lights and appliances.
  5. Excess Power Goes to Grid: Any excess electricity you produce is sent back to the grid, and thanks to net metering, you get credits on your bill.
